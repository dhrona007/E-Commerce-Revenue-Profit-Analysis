# E-Commerce Revenue & Profit Analysis

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to uncover key business insights related to **revenue trends, customer behavior, and product profitability**.

The goal is to simulate real-world data analyst workflows by performing **end-to-end data processing, analysis, and insight generation**.

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights

* Processed **5000+ orders and 12,000+ item-level records**
* Built data pipeline using **Pandas** with multi-table joins (orders, items, products)
* Calculated **accurate revenue and profit** considering discounts and costs
* Identified key business drivers such as:

  * Peak revenue periods
  * High-performing payment methods
  * Most profitable product categories

---

## 📈 Challenge 1: Revenue Analysis

Performed detailed analysis to answer critical business questions:

* Monthly revenue trends (growth/decline)
* Top revenue-generating months
* Revenue contribution by payment methods
* Average Order Value (AOV) by city and month
* Category-level profitability analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📉 Revenue drops significantly in **June**, likely due to incomplete data
* 📈 Revenue peaks in **May**, indicating strong seasonal demand or promotions
* 💳 **Netbanking** is the highest contributing payment method
* 🏙️ AOV varies across cities, indicating differences in purchasing behavior
* 👗 **Fashion category** generates the highest profit, making it a key business driver
